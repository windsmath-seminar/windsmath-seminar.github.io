WINDSMATH Poster Showcased at UCL: Embedding EDI in Education II

Presenter: Danqi Zhuang

July 19, 2024

We are delighted to announce that our WINDSMATH poster was featured at the recent University College London (UCL) event titled “Embedding Equality, Diversity, and Inclusion in Education II” on July 19, 2024. We extend our gratitude to Danqi Zhuang from the University of Strathclyde for her excellent presentation.

 

Since its inception in April last year, WINDSMATH has made significant strides. We have organized over ten online seminars, attracting upwards of 500 participants globally. Our seminars cover a wide array of topics including optimization, graph neural networks, climate change, and the mathematics of machine learning.

 

Our initiative continues to grow vibrant online communities across various platforms, witnessing an increase in followers and subscribers. We invite you to connect with us on our LinkedIn group and YouTube channel to stay updated with the latest in research and developments. 

 

WINDSMATH remains dedicated to advancing Equality, Diversity, and Inclusion (EDI) and supporting research excellence among female researchers in data science and mathematics. Discover more about our groundbreaking research and join our community to contribute to these evolving fields.
