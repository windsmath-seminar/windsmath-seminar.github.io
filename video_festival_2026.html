WINDSMATH Research Talk Video Festival 2026

About the Festival

The Women in Mathematics and Data Science Research Talk Video Festival 2026 is an online showcase celebrating high-quality research outputs from early-career female and non-binary researchers in mathematics, machine learning, and data science.

The Festival aims to increase the visibility of female and non-binary researchers while recognising excellence, innovation, and interdisciplinary impact across mathematics and data science.

This Festival is part of the Women in Data Science and Mathematics (WINDSMATH) Seminar Series, an initiative dedicated to highlighting outstanding research and fostering a more inclusive scientific community.
